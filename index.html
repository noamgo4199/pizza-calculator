üçï Classic Pizza Dough

Traditional Slow Ferment
Flour (100%): -
Preferably High Protein (>11g)
Water (65%): -
Salt (2.5%): -
Dry Yeast (0.15%): -
Olive Oil (1%): -
Sugar (1%): -
  • Mix Water (cold), Yeast, Sugar/Honey, and Olive Oil together. Let rest 5 mins.
  • Add Flour (High protein is best). Knead slowly until united.
  • Add Salt slowly while kneading.
  • Knead until smooth and separates from sides (do not overheat).
  • Perform 4 folds every 15 mins.
  • Bulk Proof: 1 hour rest as one big ball.
  • Cold Ferment: Divide into balls and place in fridge for 24 to 72 hours.
